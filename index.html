Connection Help Guide

1. Open this page on two devices.

2. Enter the host's 5-digit Room Code on the connector device, or scan their QR code.

3. Tap 'Accept' on the host device modal once prompted.

4. If connection fails, swap restrictive Wi-Fi profiles to cellular hotspots.

Privacy Audit

• Zero Storage: No files are saved online. They transfer directly to the other device.

• Secure Cryptography: Transmission uses mandatory cryptography wrappers.
