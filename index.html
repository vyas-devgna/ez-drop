Connection Help Guide

1. Open this page on two devices (desktop, phone, tablet).

2. Share the 5-digit Room Code with the secondary device.

3. Enter the code, click Connect, then click 'Accept' on the host device.

4. NAT/Firewall issue? Corporate firewalls or restrictive networks can block WebRTC direct ports. Try switching off school/work Wi-Fi to mobile data hotspots if connections continuously fail.

Privacy & Storage Audit

• zero-storage: We never store files. File data streams in slices directly through your WebRTC channel in memory. Once completed, your browser saves it down instantly to disk.

• direct encryption: WebRTC traffic uses mandated peer-to-peer DTLS/SRTP cryptography.
